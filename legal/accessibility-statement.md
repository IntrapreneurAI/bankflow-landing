# Accessibility Statement

**Effective Date:** November 3, 2024  
**Last Updated:** November 3, 2024

---

## Our Commitment to Accessibility

BankFlow.ai is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

We believe that everyone, regardless of ability, should have equal access to information and services on our website.

---

## Conformance Status

We are working to make our website conform to the **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA** standards published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

**Current Conformance Level:** Partial Conformance

We acknowledge that some parts of our website may not yet be fully accessible, and we are actively working to address these issues.

---

## Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following features to improve accessibility:

### Design and Navigation

- **Clear and consistent navigation** - Logical page structure and navigation menus
- **Descriptive headings** - Hierarchical heading structure (H1, H2, H3, etc.)
- **Skip navigation links** - Ability to skip to main content
- **Keyboard navigation** - Full keyboard accessibility without requiring a mouse

### Visual Design

- **High contrast** - Sufficient color contrast between text and background
- **Resizable text** - Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of functionality
- **Responsive design** - Website adapts to different screen sizes and devices
- **No flashing content** - Avoidance of content that flashes or blinks rapidly

### Content

- **Alternative text** - Descriptive alt text for images
- **Clear language** - Plain language and clear writing style
- **Link descriptions** - Descriptive link text (not "click here")
- **Form labels** - Clear labels for all form fields

### Multimedia

- **Video captions** - Captions for video content (when applicable)
- **Audio transcripts** - Text transcripts for audio content (when applicable)

---

## Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

- **Screen readers** - JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, TalkBack
- **Screen magnifiers** - ZoomText, Windows Magnifier
- **Speech recognition software** - Dragon NaturallySpeaking, Windows Speech Recognition
- **Keyboard-only navigation** - For users who cannot use a mouse

---

## Known Limitations

We are aware of the following accessibility limitations and are working to address them:

1. **Third-party content** - Some embedded third-party content (such as videos or widgets) may not be fully accessible
2. **PDF documents** - Some PDF documents may not be fully accessible; we are working to provide accessible alternatives
3. **Complex forms** - Some complex forms may require additional accessibility improvements
4. **Legacy content** - Older content may not meet current accessibility standards; we are reviewing and updating it

---

## Ongoing Efforts

We are committed to continuous improvement of our website's accessibility. Our ongoing efforts include:

- **Regular audits** - Conducting accessibility audits and testing
- **User testing** - Testing with users who rely on assistive technologies
- **Staff training** - Training our team on accessibility best practices
- **Content review** - Reviewing and updating content to meet accessibility standards
- **Third-party evaluation** - Engaging accessibility experts for independent evaluation

## Feedback and Assistance

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

**BankFlow.ai Accessibility Team**  
Email: accessibility@bankflow.ai  
Subject Line: "Accessibility Feedback"

When reporting an accessibility issue, please include:

- **Page URL** - The specific page where you encountered the issue
- **Description** - A detailed description of the problem
- **Assistive technology** - The assistive technology you were using (if applicable)
- **Browser and device** - Your browser and device information

We will respond to accessibility feedback within **5 business days** and work to resolve issues as quickly as possible.

Our website aims to conform to the following standards:

## Third-Party Content

Some content on our website is provided by third parties (such as embedded videos, social media widgets, or analytics tools). We do not control the accessibility of third-party content, but we strive to select accessible third-party services whenever possible.

If you encounter accessibility issues with third-party content, please let us know so we can work with the provider to address the issue or provide an alternative.

## Mobile Accessibility

Our website is designed to be accessible on mobile devices, including smartphones and tablets. We test our website on multiple devices and screen sizes to ensure a consistent and accessible experience.

If you encounter accessibility issues on a mobile device, please contact us with details about your device and the issue you experienced.
